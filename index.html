The compulsive reader.

Along with the casual, this is the reader that keeps the publishing industry alive. It is the reader who engages one reading with another and who cannot be without reading books. It may be that he dedicates himself to a single genre (see the next type) or that he is an omnivorous reader who devours all reading that falls into his hands, it does not matter. This is the type of reader who doesn't have a stack of books to read, but shelves of books to read. If you get readers of this type, you will notice it in your sales, because compulsive readers tend to associate and recommend readings. Work hard and you will be able to reach these readers.
